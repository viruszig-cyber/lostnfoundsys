About Us

Have you misplaced something valuable on campus? Don't worry, we're here to help! Our University Lost and Found System is designed to assist you in recovering your lost items and reuniting them with their rightful owners.

At our Lost and Found office, we strive to provide a reliable and efficient service to the university community. Whether you've lost your wallet, phone, keys, or any other personal belongings, we are dedicated to assisting you in the search and retrieval process.

Here's how our system works:

  1. Report a Lost Item: If you have lost something, simply check our website if someone has posted it.

  2. Claiming a Found Item: If your lost item has been found. Call him/her

  3. Update the admins, so that they can remove the item

We understand the importance of your personal belongings and the inconvenience that losing them can cause. Our goal is to make the process of finding and reclaiming lost items as smooth as possible.

 

If you have any questions or need further assistance, our friendly staff is here to assist you. Contact our Lost and Found admins, and we'll be glad to help.

Remember, losing something doesn't have to be permanent. Trust our University Lost and Found System to help you in your search for lost items and reunite you with your belongings.
